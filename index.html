Behind the bench

A garage in Athens. Way too much love.

PEACHFUZZ started in my grandmother’s carriage house with one silicone mold, two bottles of fragrance oil, and a stubborn idea: that tiny things should still be made well, by a person, with their hands, in good light.

We don’t do dropshipping. We don’t do AI scent descriptions. No glitter, no synthetic dyes, nothing that doesn’t belong in the air you breathe for the next forty-five days.

Quietly answered

Frequent questions.

How long do they last?

Forty-five days of strong scent throw, plus another two-to-three weeks of softer base notes. Keep them out of direct sun and they’ll go longer.

Do you ship outside Georgia?

Anywhere in the lower forty-eight. USPS Ground Advantage, two-to-five business days. Heat-safe packaging from June through September because Southern summers.

Are your fragrances safe?

Phthalate-free, IFRA-compliant, sourced from two US suppliers I’ve visited in person. We don’t formulate for ingestion or skin contact — these are for the air in your cabin.

Can you match a scent I already love?

Often. Tell me the candle, cologne, or memory and I’ll send a small sample pour before committing to a full batch.

Do you wholesale?

Yes — minimum twelve pouches, tiered pricing kicks in at fifty. Use the custom form and tell me about the shop.
