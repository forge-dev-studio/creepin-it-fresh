Frequent questions.

How long do they last?

Forty-five days of strong scent throw, plus another two-to-three weeks of softer base notes. Keep them out of direct sun and they’ll go longer.

Do you ship outside Georgia?

Anywhere in the lower forty-eight. USPS Ground Advantage, two-to-five business days. Heat-safe packaging from June through September because Southern summers.

Are your fragrances safe?

Phthalate-free, IFRA-compliant, sourced from two US suppliers I’ve visited in person. We don’t formulate for ingestion or skin contact — these are for the air around you.

Can you match a scent I already love?

Often. Tell me the candle, cologne, or memory and I’ll send a small sample pour before committing to a full batch.

Do you wholesale?

Yes — minimum twelve pouches, tiered pricing kicks in at fifty. Use the custom form and tell me about the shop.
